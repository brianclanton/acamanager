Group Member Database Schema

General
> username - Same username as email account

Info
> first_name - Member first name
> last_name - Member last name
> year - Year in school
> graduation_year - Year of graduation
> voice_part - General voice part
> role - Role within group
> hometown_city - Hometown city
> hometown_state - Hometown state

Social Media
> website - Website url
> facebook - Facebook url
> twitter - Twitter url

Contact
> cell - Cell phone number
> main_email - Non group email

Attendance
> gig_attendance - Number of gigs attendended
> rehearsal_attendance - Number of rehearsal attended
